💡 How This Works

  • IndexedDB Persistence: Posts are stored in your browser's IndexedDB
  • Refresh the page: Your data persists!
  • Inspect storage: Open DevTools → Application → IndexedDB → "blog"
  • Clear data: Delete the IndexedDB database in DevTools to reset
